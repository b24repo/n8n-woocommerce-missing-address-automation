WORKFLOW PURPOSE
----------------
Automates handling of incomplete shipping addresses reported by 3PL warehouses.
The workflow:
  - Receives webhook notifications from 3PL systems
  - Extracts order/customer data using Claude AI
  - Validates orders against WooCommerce
  - Identifies missing address fields
  - Sends customer notifications (Email + SMS)
  - Alerts fulfillment team (Slack)
  - Logs all actions (Google Sheets)
  - Includes comprehensive error handling
